A Review of Different Approaches for Visual Question-Answering Systems

Visual Question Answering (VQA) is an AI-based system where for a given pair of question and image, the system attempts to give a relevant answer. This is usually a combination of image processing and understanding the question to match both to deliver the output. VQA can be efficiently used for scenarios where identification and classification of the images are needed.
